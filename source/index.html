Me Myself, and I

I am a Computer Scientest with a passion for web. I've been tinkering with all things technology from a young age, like most developers. I grew up in Nottingham, where I studied Computing, Photography and Maths at College. I am currently working as a Web/Multimedia Front end Developer at Xerox, as part of my Computer Science degree at the University of Hull.

Working at Xerox

I am currently Working at Xerox as a Web/Multimedia Front End Developer, as part of my University Course. My role is very hands on, and varied. I am very fortunate to work in a very skilled agile web team, and to be treated as a core member and not an intern.

So far, I have built several custom interactive components including those on the xerox about page. I also built the landing page for a fortune 500 company from the ground up, which was live on their primary domain(conduent.com) and viewed by key stakeholders. I have also worked on lots of smaller projects for various email campaigns and product launches.

University of Hull

I have been a student at the University of Hull for the past 3 years, studying Computer Science. I have made life long friends, experienced THE NORTH and broadened my knowledge on the wide variety of fields within the Computer Science domain. I already felt web development was the topic I wanted to focus on, but the '2D Graphics and User Interface Design' module really reinforced that. Comparing the various fields in Computer Science gave me a lot of confidence in the industry I chose, allowing me to compare and contrast across modules.

Working at TNS

During my studies in Hull, I worked as a 'Phone Interviewer' in Hull's TNS call-center. I developed a distinct telephone voice, and learnt a lot about the views of the UK population. This gave me great insight in to working life, and taught me many life lessons. I also gained a lot of confidence and vocal ability from the experience.
